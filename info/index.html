Welcome to goat's cafe!

Hi, I'm goat! I'm an artist, writer, and sucker for good stories. Visual language is how I feel most confident expressing myself. I've been drawing for as long as I can remember; it's my first and longest love, long before I had my first computer (let alone internet access). Exposure to art-sharing websites like deviantART and tumblr did nothing to stifle that spark. 

GOATS.CAFE is my personal static website. I'd call it an online business card, but that sounds lame as hell. This is my little corner of the internet and a reminder that I am allowed to take up space. Thank you for your stay, I hope you find something worth your while :)
