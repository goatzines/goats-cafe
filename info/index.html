Hi, I'm goat! I'm an artist, writer, and sucker for good stories. I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. It's my first and longest love, long before I had my first computer (let alone internet access); exposure (and addiction) to sites like deviantART and tumblr did nothing to stifle that spark. 

GOATS.CAFE is my personal static website; I'd call it an online business card, but that's lame as hell. This is my little corner of the internet and an excercise in taking up space. Thank you for your stay, I hope you find something worth your while :)
